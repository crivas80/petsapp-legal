Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 19, 2026

CSIL Technology ("we", "our", "us") operates the pets.app mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use pets.app:

  • Account information: email address, name, last name, and profile photo.
  • Pet information: name, species, breed, age, health conditions, vaccination records, and photos of your pets.
  • Location data: approximate location (only when you use the veterinary clinics map feature, with your explicit permission).
  • Community content: posts, comments, stories, adoption listings, and chat messages you create.
  • Purchase data: order history and cart contents.
  • Device information: push notification tokens for sending you reminders and notifications.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account.
  • To provide pet health tracking, vaccination reminders, and QR-based pet identification.
  • To display nearby veterinary clinics on a map.
  • To enable the social community features (posts, stories, adoption listings, chats).
  • To process product purchases through the marketplace.
  • To send push notifications about vaccination reminders and app activity.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate the app:

  • Google Firebase: authentication, database (Firestore), and cloud functions. Firebase Privacy Policy.
  • Cloudinary: image storage and delivery. Cloudinary Privacy Policy.
  • Expo: push notifications and app infrastructure. Expo Privacy Policy.

4. Biometric Data

pets.app offers optional biometric login (fingerprint/Face ID). Biometric data is processed entirely on your device using the operating system's secure APIs. We never collect, store, or transmit biometric data to our servers.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely in Google Firebase (Firestore) with encryption in transit and at rest. We implement security rules to ensure users can only access their own data. Cart data is stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We only share data as described in the Third-Party Services section above, which is necessary for the app to function.

Exception — Law enforcement and animal protection: In cases of suspected animal trafficking, abuse, cruelty, illegal sales, or any violation of animal protection laws (including the Venezuelan Ley para la Protección de la Fauna Doméstica, Libre y en Cautiverio, Ley Penal del Ambiente, and CITES), CSIL Technology reserves the right to disclose user data — including personal information, communications, IP addresses, and activity logs — to competent authorities, law enforcement agencies, and animal protection organizations without prior notice to the user, as permitted by applicable law.

7. Children's Privacy

pets.app is not intended for children under 13 years of age, in compliance with COPPA. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected data from a child under 13, we will delete it promptly.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data through your profile settings.
  • Update your information at any time via the app.
  • Delete your account and all associated data through Settings > Delete Account. This action permanently removes your data from our systems, including: profile, pets, posts, comments, stories, adoptions, orders, notifications, chats, and follow relationships.
  • Contact us to request information about your data.

9. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, all personal data is permanently removed from our systems. Community content (stories) automatically expires after 24 hours.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes through the app. Continued use of pets.app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
