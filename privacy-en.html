Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 8, 2026

CSIL Technology ("we", "our", "us") operates the pets.app mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use pets.app:

  • Account information: email address, name, last name, profile photo, and assigned role (customer, associate, store, veterinarian, clinic, redactor, admin).
  • Pet information: name, species, breed, age, weight, health conditions, vaccination records, medical history, and photos of your pets.
  • Location data: approximate location (only when you use the veterinary clinics map or report a lost pet, with your explicit permission); state/city declared when creating a product listing.
  • Community content: posts, comments, stories, adoption listings, lost-pet reports, chat messages, and reactions you create.
  • Marketplace data: cart contents, order history, shipping addresses, courier preferences (MRW, Zoom, Tealca, Liberty, Domesa), receipts, and ratings you give or receive.
  • Device information: Expo push notification tokens used to send reminders and notifications.
  • Optional payment data: if you choose to save payment methods (Pago Móvil, bank transfer, etc.), only the descriptive label is stored. We do not store full card numbers, CVV, or banking credentials.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account.
  • To provide pet health tracking, vaccination reminders, and QR-based pet identification.
  • To display nearby veterinary clinics on a map.
  • To enable the social community features (posts, stories, adoption listings, lost-pet reports, chats).
  • To process product purchases through the marketplace, including filtering by city/state and shipping options.
  • To send push notifications about vaccination reminders, chat messages, order updates, and app activity.
  • To display the daily BCV exchange rate (USD ↔ Bs) for purchases in Venezuela.
  • To generate personalized educational content (tips) using AI based on your pets' species/breed (no personal identifiers are sent to the AI provider).

4. Biometric Data

pets.app offers optional biometric login (fingerprint/Face ID). Biometric data is processed entirely on your device using the operating system's secure APIs (Apple Face ID/Touch ID, Android BiometricPrompt). We never collect, store, or transmit biometric data to our servers.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely in Google Firebase (Firestore) with encryption in transit and at rest. We implement Firestore Security Rules to ensure users can only access their own data and field-level restrictions for sensitive operations (e.g., product stock, adoption checklists). Cart data is stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We only share data as described in the Third-Party Services section above, which is necessary for the app to function.

Exception — Law enforcement and animal protection: In cases of suspected animal trafficking, abuse, cruelty, illegal sales, or any violation of animal protection laws (including the Venezuelan Ley para la Protección de la Fauna Doméstica, Libre y en Cautiverio, Ley Penal del Ambiente, and CITES), CSIL Technology reserves the right to disclose user data — including personal information, communications, IP addresses, and activity logs — to competent authorities, law enforcement agencies, and animal protection organizations without prior notice to the user, as permitted by applicable law.

7. Children's Privacy

pets.app is not intended for children under 13 years of age, in compliance with COPPA. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected data from a child under 13, we will delete it promptly.

8. Veterinary Disclaimer

Tips, articles, reminders, AI-generated content, and any health information shown in pets.app are informational and educational only. They do not constitute veterinary diagnosis, treatment, or professional advice and do not replace consultation with a licensed veterinarian. Always consult your veterinarian before making medical decisions for your pet.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data through your profile settings.
  • Update your information at any time via the app.
  • Delete your account and all associated data — see our Account Deletion page for step-by-step instructions and the data retention policy.
  • Contact us to request information about your data or to exercise any of these rights.

10. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, all personal data is permanently removed, except for the limited anonymized records described on the Account Deletion page (completed orders for tax compliance, anonymized reviews, abuse reports). Community stories automatically expire after 24 hours.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes through the app. Continued use of pets.app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
