Delete my pets.app account

Last updated: June 8, 2026

This page explains how to request deletion of your account and associated data in pets.app, operated by CSIL Technology. It complies with Google Play account deletion requirements (effective since 2024).

Option 1 — From the app (recommended)

  1. Open the pets.app application and sign in with your account.
  2. Go to the Profile tab.
  3. Tap Settings.
  4. Scroll to the Account section and tap Delete account.
  5. Confirm the action. Your account and all associated data will be deleted immediately.

Option 2 — By email

If you cannot access the app, send an email to petsapp.csil@gmail.com with the subject "Account deletion request" and include:

  • Your registered email in pets.app.
  • Your full name as shown in your profile.
  • (Optional) The reason for deletion, to help us improve.

We will process your request within 7 business days and send you a confirmation email when complete.

What data is deleted?

When you delete your account we permanently remove:

  • Your profile (email, name, photo, role).
  • Your pets (photos, medical history, vaccines, reminders, QR codes).
  • Your posts, comments, stories, "paw" reactions in the community.
  • Your individual chats and messages.
  • Your adoption listings and lost-pet reports.
  • Your cart and pending unfinished orders.
  • Your follow relationships and notification subscriptions.
  • Your open role requests (associate, vet, clinic).
  • Your published products if you are an associate/store (completed orders are anonymized).

What data is retained and for how long?

For legal, accounting, and security reasons we retain, in anonymized form when possible:

  • Completed orders (without your name or email) for up to 5 years, to comply with tax obligations and resolve disputes with sellers.
  • Public reviews you left for sellers are kept without your identity (shown as "Deleted user").
  • Reports of abuse, animal trafficking, or Terms violations you made or received, until the case is closed and handed to the appropriate authorities (see Terms & Conditions, section 2.3).
  • Anonymized security logs (IP, timestamps) for up to 90 days, for fraud detection.

Is it reversible?

No. Deletion is permanent and irreversible. If you wish to use pets.app again in the future, you will need to create a new account.
